First Stride is a natural extension of our commitment to Growing the Game. In partnership with Bauer, we’re giving children and their parents the chance to experience hockey for the first time with very little financial commitment.
First Stride’s mission is to help family’s tryout the sport of hockey by reducing equipment cost as a barrier to trial. We believe, if given even just one chance at playing, our youth will likely become players for life.
  • Register for your local First Stride event
  • Attend your personal fitting session at Total Hockey
  • Leave fitting session with your freet gear*
  • Go to the FREE event and enjoy!
  • Before leaving the First Stride event sign up for your associations LTP program
*Everything but skates is provided. A credit card is required at the time of fitting but you will not be charged. Simply sign up for LTP or return the equipment after your First Stride on ice session.
We’ve (Total Hockey has?) partnered with Bauer and associations across the country to get more kids playing hockey. We take care of everything from registration to ice time. All you need to do is register, get fitted and show up at the event. Simple.
